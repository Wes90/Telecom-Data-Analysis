Dashboard

For this dashboard, I've created visuals mainly focusing on factors that may be closely related to the client churn and using a machine learning model to predict churn based on the actual data.
This information is important to gain forsight on our clients behavior based on the type of subscribtion.


This multiple pie chart focuses on Internet service, phone service, and gender.
Looking at this chart, we can almost rule out gender being a significant factor, though looking at phone service paired with fiber optic service, the churn percentage is much greater while also having the majority of clients. Could this churn rate be caused by price or customer service?


Reviewing the privious charts narrowed down the analysis to technical support with users and total charges of services.
This stacked bar char shows the internet service with and without tech support and reveals the percent of total churn for each service. This clearly shows the positive impact tech support has with keeping clients subscribed and providing more support will greatly help keep them long term.
